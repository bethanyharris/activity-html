The Ugliest Shark in the Sea

The Goblin Shark

The Goblin Shark is one of the strangest looking creatures in the sea, and is still not fully understood by the scientists who study it. Measuring 3-4 meters in length, this shark has uncharacteristically small teeth for a shark of it's size. Scientists have been unable to learn a great deal about this species due to the fact that it lives at depths greater than 100ft, making it harder for scientists to aquire specimens. The International Union for Conservation of Nature(IUNC) has deemed the goblin shark a conservation status of Least Concern, meaning the goblin shark remains to be an unthreatened species...
